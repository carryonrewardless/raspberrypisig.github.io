Problems

  • Print not sticking to bed
  • Print warping or stringy
  • Filament jams

Solutions

  • Level Bed (use dial indicator)
  • Buildtak/ PEI sheet (eg. http://www.aurarum.com.au/accessories/334-flasforge-build-surface-similar-to-buildtak.html)
